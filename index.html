Our Story

About V MORGEN & SOEHNE

A newly established construction company built on experience, integrity, and an unwavering commitment to quality across Kenya.

Who We Are

Building with Purpose

V MORGEN & SOEHNE LTD is a dynamic construction company headquartered in Nairobi, Kenya. Though newly established, our team brings deep hands-on expertise across residential, commercial, and civil infrastructure projects.

Our name reflects a blend of vision — "Morgen" meaning tomorrow in German — and the spirit of family legacy in building Kenya's future structures together.

We operate across Nairobi & Central Kenya, the Rift Valley, and are expanding our footprint nationwide, combining technical precision with client-centred delivery on every project.

Our Mission

To deliver innovative, sustainable, and high-quality construction solutions that exceed client expectations while contributing to the development of Kenya's built environment.

Our Vision

To be a leading construction company in East Africa, recognized for integrity, excellence, and transformative infrastructure that shapes communities for generations.

Leadership

Meet the Founder

Vincent Kioko
Founder & Director

Vincent Kioko is the visionary behind V MORGEN & SOEHNE LTD. With a hands-on background in construction management, civil works, and client relations, Vincent founded the company with the mission of delivering high-quality, dependable construction services across Kenya. He leads the company's strategic direction, project oversight, and client engagement — ensuring every project reflects the company's commitment to excellence, innovation, and integrity.

Commercial Construction

From commercial fit-outs to large-scale paving works, we deliver high-quality commercial solutions with minimal disruption to live environments.
